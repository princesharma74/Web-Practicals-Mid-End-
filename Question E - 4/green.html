Green

Green is the third color present in the National Flag of India. It is seen in the bottommost portion of the flag and occupies one-fourth of the total flag area. The green color in the tricolor symbolizes a variety of meanings, including faith and fertility.

The green color has been traditionally used as a symbol of fertility in almost all cultures around the world. The symbolism of this color has been adopted in the Indian National Flag to signify that the nation will always be fertile and overflowing with resources and wealth.

The green color also symbolizes peace and prosperity. Since India is a diverse nation full of many different religions and cultures, it is extremely important to ensure that peace, harmony and unity are maintained in the country. The green color in the flag serves as a reminder to all its citizens to continue striving for peace, faith, prosperity and unity.

The green color also serves to represent the growth of the nation both in terms of its population and its economy. The green stripe in the flag symbolizes the optimism and hope of the nation for the future.
